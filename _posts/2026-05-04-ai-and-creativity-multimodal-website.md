I created [AI and Creativity: A Double-Edged Sword]({{ "/multimodal-project/" | relative_url }}) as a multimodal website that explores how generative AI reshapes creative work.

The website combines research synthesis, visual comparison, motion, and source-backed argumentation to make a complex question easier to enter: AI can expand ideation and accelerate creative production, but it may also narrow originality, collective diversity, and authorship. Rather than treating AI as either a replacement for creative work or a neutral tool, I frame it as part of a co-creative system where human judgment, selection, and refinement still matter.
